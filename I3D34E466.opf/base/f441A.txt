ty and not insisting on opinions derived from personal authorities;realizing correctly the reality of the Buddha;realizi

ally, attaining peace by stopping everything from ignorance to old age, death, sorrow, lamentation, misery, anxiety, and

ation;and realizing that living beings know no end to their views (concerning these twelve links of dependent originatio
